List of Russian federal subjects by GDP per capita

Tyumen Oblast has largest GRDP per capita in Russia of around US$ 54,000 while Ingushetia has lowest of around US$ 2,000.[1] In 2022 Moscow GRDP per capita reached US$ 32,000 while Saint Petersburg stood at US$ 29,000.[1]

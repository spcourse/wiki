List of Italian regions by GRDP per capita

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
(Redirected from List of Italian regions by GRP per capita)

This article is about the gross regional domestic product (GRDP) per capita of Italian regions in nominal values.[1] Values are shown in EUR€. For easy comparison, all the GRDP figures are converted into US$ according to annual average exchange rates.[2][n 1] All values are rounded to the nearest hundred.

Note: Trentino-South Tyrol has separate values measured for the two self-governing provinces that make up the region—the Province of Trento, commonly known as Trentino, and the Province of Bolzano, commonly known as South Tyrol. These are shown in the table below in italics.

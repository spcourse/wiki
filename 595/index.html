List of countries by merchandise exports

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

For a list of countries by merchandise exports also showing merchandise imports and the resulting trade balance, see List of countries by net goods exports.

The following article lists different countries and territories by their merchandise exports according to data from the World Bank and other sources. Merchandise exports are goods that are produced in one country and sold to another country. Only physical objects are counting under this kind of exports. For example, cars, clothing, machinery, and agricultural products are merchandise exports. Exports of services are excluded.
